The Revolutionary Vision

Michael Potash

Visual Documentarian & Historical Interpreter

Like the brave souls who fought at Lexington and Concord, I believe in capturing moments that define the American spirit. My professional journey began in the historic streets of New York City, where the echoes of revolution still resonate, and continued at Western Washington University where I documented the competitive spirit that mirrors our nation's fighting determination.

Currently dividing time between the Pacific Northwest and the original theater of independence, I specialize in visual storytelling that honors both our revolutionary past and dynamic present.
